About the Institute

HIET GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS is located in Village Shahpur in Kangra district of Himachal Pradesh. The institution was established in 2010 by Dhalaudhar Education Society. Institution is surrounded by breathtaking beauty of Dhauladhar Range which adds to the lush green and beautiful campus making it most conducive for studies. It proposes to encourage active interaction of the students with industry and a mission to serve the society.
